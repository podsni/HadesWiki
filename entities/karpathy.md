# Andrej Karpathy

Computer scientist, educator, and former OpenAI / Tesla research lead. Author of
the [[llm-wiki-pattern|LLM Wiki pattern]] idea file that this wiki is built from.

## Key Facts

- Author of the original "LLM Wiki" gist, which is the **canonical source** of the
  pattern. ^[raw/articles/karpathy-llm-wiki.md]
- Origin of the framing "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is
  the codebase." ^[raw/articles/karpathy-llm-wiki.md]
- Origin of the framing "The wiki is a persistent, compounding artifact." ^[raw/articles/karpathy-llm-wiki.md]
- Connects the pattern to [[memex|Vannevar Bush's Memex (1945)]] as a historical
  antecedent — and identifies that the unsolved Memex problem (who does the
  maintenance?) is now solved by LLMs. ^[raw/articles/karpathy-llm-wiki.md]
